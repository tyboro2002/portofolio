Chess Fish

ChessFish is a high-performance chess engine developed in C++, designed for speed and tactical accuracy. The core of the engine is built on a Bitboard board representation, which maps the 64 squares of the chessboard to 64-bit integers. This allows the engine to use lightning-fast bitwise operations such as shifts and logical AND/OR to calculate legal moves, evaluate board occupancy, and detect pins or checks in a very low amount of CPU cycles.
